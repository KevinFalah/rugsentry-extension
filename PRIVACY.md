# Privacy Policy

Rugsentry ("we", "our", or "the Software") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains our practices regarding data.

## 1. Zero Data Collection
Rugsentry is a "Zero-Knowledge" extension. We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data, including:
*   IP addresses
*   Browsing history (outside of the specified DEX platforms)
*   Wallet addresses or private keys
*   Geolocation data

## 2. Data Processing
To provide real-time security scores, Rugsentry only processes **Contract Addresses (CA)** from the URLs of supported DEX platforms (DexScreener, Birdeye, and Pump.fun). 
*   These addresses are sent directly to public security APIs (GoPlus Security, RugCheck).
*   We do not store these addresses on any external server.

## 3. Local Storage
All settings (like "Show Floating Badge") and your local scan history are stored exclusively on your device using Chrome's `localStorage`. We have no access to this data.

## 4. Third-Party Services
Rugsentry interacts with third-party APIs. We encourage users to review the privacy policies of GoPlus Security and RugCheck.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected in this document on GitHub.
